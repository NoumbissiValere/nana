Blessing Ceremony of ABEBOH

by abeboh

The Blessing Ceremony of ABEBOH took place on Monday 12th September 2016. It was a call for the blessing of the occasion. Rev. Fr. Christophe came in from the University Parish to bless the Building.
It was an official kick start of ABEBOH . After the blessing ceremony which ended with a light refreshment, The CEO turned to Facebook the next day to do a post with the following message "Hello All. We were so happy on the massive turn out for the blessing ceremony of the ABEBOH Building. We did not expect this numbers. ABEHOH is for you. let us create Technology in Afrca.
Thanks to Family, Friends, Classmates and Community that showed the concern.
Glory be to God." .
